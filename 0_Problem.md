# Problem Statement
The rapid growth of agentic AI systems has created new opportunities to automate complex software engineering tasks, but research in this area is still fragmented. There is limited comprehensive understanding of the types of agent-based AI systems used in software engineering and their underlying architectures and methods. Additionally, evaluation standards, metrics, and experimental validation approaches are inconsistent across studies. The field also lacks systematic analysis of challenges, limitations, and research gaps. Therefore, there is a need for a systematic literature review to consolidate existing knowledge and provide insights into current trends and future research directions in agentic AI for software engineering tasks.
