# Research Objective
- RO1: To identify and classify the types of agent-based or autonomous AI systems applied to software engineering tasks and analyze the methods, frameworks, and architectures used in these systems.
- RO2: To examine how agent-based AI systems are evaluated in software engineering applications, including evaluation techniques, metrics, and experimental settings used in the literature.
- RO3: To analyze the limitations, challenges, and open research questions associated with applying agent-based AI systems to software engineering tasks.
